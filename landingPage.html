Section 4: More Information

Built from the search for streetwear with Filipino identity.

LikhaTela started from a problem we personally noticed as students and online shoppers. We wanted to find streetwear that looked modern but still had a Filipino identity. However, most of the designs we usually saw online were either foreign-inspired or came from different local shops scattered across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms.

Because of this, buying or customizing streetwear was not always easy. We had to browse different pages, message sellers one by one, ask for prices, wait for replies, and sometimes use another platform just to complete an order. At the same time, we also noticed that many local Filipino designers have creative and meaningful designs, but they do not always get enough attention online.

This made us realize that the problem was not only about convenience. It was also about giving Filipino creativity a better space to be seen and appreciated. Through this experience, we started to see fashion not just as clothing, but as a way to express culture, identity, and local pride.

That is how LikhaTela came to be. "Likha" represents creativity, while "Tela" represents fabric. Together, LikhaTela means creating clothing that carries Filipino-inspired stories and designs. This journey encouraged us to create a platform that supports local designers and helps customers wear something that feels stylish, meaningful, and proudly Filipino.

Company Name LokalThread Creative Co.

Purpose

The purpose of LikhaTela is to provide a centralized mobile platform where customers can discover, customize, and purchase Filipino-inspired streetwear more easily. It aims to solve the problem of scattered sellers, inconvenient ordering processes, and limited exposure for local Filipino designers.

LikhaTela also aims to promote Filipino culture through fashion. By giving local designers a space to showcase their work, the platform helps support creativity, self-expression, and local pride. More than just selling clothes, LikhaTela wants to build a community where Filipino-inspired streetwear can be appreciated, supported, and worn with meaning.

Vision

To become a trusted Filipino streetwear platform that helps local designers share their creativity while allowing customers to wear fashion that represents culture, identity, and local pride.

Mission

LokalThread Creative Co. aims to develop LikhaTela as a convenient mobile app where users can discover, customize, and purchase Filipino-inspired streetwear in one place. We are committed to supporting local designers by giving them more digital exposure and opportunities to showcase their work. Through our platform, we want to make streetwear more meaningful by connecting fashion with Filipino culture and creativity.
